This calculator allows you to estimate the customer lifetime value (CLV) in the the simplest way; when the sales cycle is not too complicated you can approximate the calculation by using the 'turnover', 'number of customers', and 'churn rate' (% of customers that stop buying from you every month).

If you need a more accurate value, you can do so by inputing the profit margin (% Gross margin), and your financing interest rate (you can use your APR: eg. 5% for loans, 25% for credit card, etc).
